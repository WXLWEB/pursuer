Dmg is a focus on sports fields Technology Co., Ltd. in June 2014 to develop a tennis ball on O2O APP (android, ios). Pursuer designed to provide a full UI, interactive design technology for the Grand Slam.

Design and Analysis
User analysis
Tennis crowd age is 25-45 years old, according to the previous analysis, he or she had the main demand for the product: simple, elegant, beautiful, attention to user experience of the product.
Design development trend analysis
Design itself is no absolute good or bad, but it is an inevitable trend in the flat, not only because of fatigue caused to change, but mainly because of the increasingly high degree of digitization in daily life caused people computer and mobile devices become more familiar with the lower costs of learning, so materialized slowly fade effect. According to the analysis tennis crowd Age: 25-45 years of age (as shown), for computers and mobile phones are more familiar with, learning costs for them to learn the cost is not the most important, it is recommended to use a flat design.
Adaptation analysis
With iPhone6 (750 * 1334px) and iPhone6 Plus (1242 * 2208px) launch, iphone adapter design sets a minimum figure iPhone5 and iPhone6Plus, iPhone4 and iPhone5 because of the same width, but the height of a little shorter, iPhone6 is iPhone5 proportional amplification, and Because Xcode6 support SVG format, iPhone4, iPhone5, iPhone6 can share a set of design drawings. However, due to 401ppi pixel ratio iPhone6Plus true about 2.60, in order to solve this problem, Apple has a whole new concept renderedpixels rendered pixel, if the pixel ratio is 3X, then theoretically a CSS width to 414px 1242px screen should have the physical pixel (in reality is 1080 px, 13% smaller), so it is necessary to design a separate design. Android phones are very much different sized devices, and how to do that adaptation? According to our many years of experience in designing a project can fit a small 480 * 800px resolution, 720 * 1280px can fit a high-resolution, if resources allow, you can design a 1080 * 1920px screen.
HMI analysis
Comfortable fingers minimum size Research
Phone, pad mode of operation is divided into two types: one-hand operation (mainly on the thumb touching the screen) and two-handed operation (mainly on the index finger touching the screen), click on the size of the smallest single-handed operation is 108px (near 9mm); both hands to operate the smallest Click size is 88px (close to 7mm).
Thumb hotspot
“Tapworthy” findings further studies, we know that in the natural bent thumb, the best, the flexibility of movement of the finger is relatively good stability of the handset. Reasonable right thumb operated "hot zone", roughly showing a "fan ring" area.
Logo design
logo design inspiration comes mainly from Zhuanti different actions and tennis player of traditional Chinese culture, calligraphy performance by Zhuanti way to express tennis player attitude, skillfully Chinese traditional culture and the combination of tennis, so the design has local culture.
Wireframes
Pre wireframes, mainly used to discuss the big design direction of the product: Use process, operating rationality, information framework, facilitate communication between designers, engineers and customers, early detection and solve problems, help to improve product quality and save product development cycle.
