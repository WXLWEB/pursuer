Love Technology is a provider of online health care technology platform services mainly doctors and patients after diagnosis, patient follow-up and attention to hospital after health management, and focus the field of oncology. Love Technology attaches great importance to the user experience and brand image of the product, find us together in 2013 to work together. Compulsive depending on design requirements and design goals Love Technology and its brand planning, VI design, web side, mobile terminal IOS / android Service package design.

User analysis
Users of this product can be divided into two categories: the doctor and the patient (after the main diagnosis), doctors usually work very busy, pressure, expectations for the product: high efficiency, simple and refreshing, easy to use; patients usually psychological pressure, irritability, depression, product look: simple and elegant, easy to use, emotional care.
Color analysis
Extraction and analysis of the product color: color is divided into a positive symbol of warm colors and cool colors, warm colors: warm, sexy, authoritative, confident; negative symbol: gore, violence, jealousy, control, impulse. Positive sign of cool colors: fresh, quiet, hope, ideals, trust and authority; negative symbol: cold, passivity.
Black symbolizes authority, elegant, low-key, creative; also means perseverance, apathy, defense; white symbolizes purity, sanctity, kindness, trust and openness. So this product is absolutely not the color black large area applications, but the application white.
After the above analysis, it is mainly the product color with blue and white, conveys give the user a comfortable, quiet, elegant, simple and elegant visual experience, reduce patient anxiety and improve efficiency in the use of patients and physicians.
