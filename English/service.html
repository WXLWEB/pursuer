Service

Service Range

  • Interactive Design

    The main analysis of interaction design , definition and design of the user's behavior , focusing on product availability , ease of use, low-fidelity prototypes by simulation and show the user a series of acts , from which the optimal solution propose.

  • Ui Design

    Ui refers to human-computer interaction design software / products , operating logic , the overall appearance of the interface design . To define the design based on user characteristics , design the content and objectives , icons, perform color , typography, such as a comprehensive design plate.

  • Industrial Design

    Industrial design means in order to achieve a particular purpose , from concept to build a practical implementation , and use explicit behavior means that out of the series . It contains all the use of modern methods of production and Service design process. Simply put : it achieve the perfect combination of form and function , creating business value for the enterprise

  • Branding

    By breakdown products , market segments, sub-groups , to help companies find their own brand positioning in the industry , to achieve valuable personalized brand strategy , establish a good brand image in the minds of consumers .
