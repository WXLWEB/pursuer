Responsive Web Design

——Depending on the width of the structural adjustment of the size of intelligent terminals, to ensure the best reading results, thereby improving the user experience of products.

What is responsive design ?

As smart phones increasingly high coverage , pc end more different resolutions , in order to improve the user experience and different sizes of different terminal devices , responsive Web design (Responsive Web Design) concept is presented Ethan Marcotte, is provide a variety of devices can be read carefully a web design approach , RWD make your web page to show a different design styles in different devices . It is mainly achieved through the flow layout flexibly change the page with different sizes according to the different devices , to achieve the best reading results .

How to define the breakpoints ?

What breakpoint is ? Simple description is that the width of the critical point of the device , which is more concerned about what is in front of everyone in the Medial Queries min-width and max-width value is ? Then Responsive design, there are three kinds of common breakpoint our future conditional Medial Queries can be based on these three breakpoints to be.

  1. The first major break for small-screen smartphones, their width is less than480px,W<480px;
  2. Second breakpoint mainly for large screen smartphone and mobile devices, their width is greater than or equal 480px and less than 768px,480px≤W<768px;
  3. The third major break for the tablet (pad), their width is greater than or equal 768px and less than or equal 1024px,768px≤W≤1024px;
  4. The fourth breakpoint main target in the computer (PC), their width is larger than 1024px,W>1024px; For a more perfect fit different mobile terminals, in the design and development process, we can increase and decrease depending on the project requirements breakpoint;

Responsive website design and flow lines

Step 1: Determine requires a compatible device type, screen size

Responsive web design (RWD) is an approach to web design aimed at crafting sites to provide an optimal viewing experience;

Step 2 : Make wireframe prototypes

Determined for several different size wireframe prototypes were produced , need to think clearly different sizes , how to change the layout of the page , the content size to zoom, cut features, content , or even for a special environment for the specialized design and so on;

Step 3：Test wireframe prototype

Import pictures to the appropriate equipment for some simple tests , early detection can help us accessibility, readability and other issues exist;

Step 4: visual design

Note that the screen pixel density of mobile devices and traditional computer screen is not the same , in the design of the need to ensure that the contents of the text more readable, controls such as clickable area of ​​the region;

Step 5:Front-end implementation

Compared with the traditional web development , responsive design pages due to page layout , content size has changed , so the final output is more likely to access and design draft larger front-end developers and designers need to communicate more.
